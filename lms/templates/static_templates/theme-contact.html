Everyday at Labster, we’re inspired
by the big picture of science education.

As an internationally focused company, we’re driven by the belief that all students should be able to access the awe-inspiring study of life-science. That’s why we created a multimedia, virtual approach that optimizes and supports traditional lab instruction.  Proven to significantly enhance student’s motivation and engagement, our pioneering teaching tools are developed with both world-class learning and cost-reduction in mind.

Labster partners with top universities and their leading faculty to create and develop innovative, scientifically advanced laboratories. Among these contributors are Stanford University, The Technical University of Denmark, Novo Nordisk, Hong Kong University, and many others in academia and professional industries.

Incepted with a entrepreneurial spirit, we value strong relationships among partners, investors, teammates, and collaborators. This is evidenced by our numerous awards including a two-time Venture Cup win and funding from the Danish Market Development Fund as well as the Danish Ministry of Science. Our products are used by more than 10,000 students globally; a number that’s rapidly increasing every day.

We believe that a collaborative, motivational working environment is the key to innovation. With a diverse staff throughout our offices in Copenhagen, Denmark and Bali, Indonesia, our company culture is one of leadership, contribution, and integrity.

Contact us!

Through both my own studies and teaching experience, I had observed that laboratory teaching was both very limited by budget, time and safety...when I found that available research on the subject supported my observations, I set out to create a laboratory simulator where students could perform expensive, hazardous experiments not possible in a real lab environment, with proven effectiveness in regards to student learning and motivation.

Mads Tvillinggaard Bonde

Founder and CEO, Labster
