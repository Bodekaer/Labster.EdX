Open Positions

Entrepreneur Lifestyle

With a distinctly entrepreneurial vibe, working at Labster will grow your capacity as a leader. Our team is driven, hardworking, idea-heavy and responsible, and we love to inspire each other to grow and learn. What else would you expect from a company that’s making next-generation science education solutions?

Founders House

We share desks and tips with the best startup gurus in the business at Founders House in Copenhagen. At the very center of Copenhagen, in Founders House you can turn to your left for coding help from your developer neighbor, or to your right for marketing strategies from self-made CMOs. But in addition to our knowledge pool, Founders House has fun! FIFA tournaments, friday beer and in-house competitions are not uncommon.

Small company = Small hierarchy

We’re a small team, which means a flat hierarchy. It’s a working environment Denmark is known for, but Labster encourages it tenfold. You’ll undoubtedly have lasting, important input on the direction of the company when you get to talk with the CEO daily.

Flexible working hours and travel

We believe in flexible work hours and a good work-life balance -we are interested in the output and having happy co-workers. Our development team is located at Bali, Indonesia, and is led by co-founder Michael Bodekaer. So be prepared for travelling if you join Labster!
